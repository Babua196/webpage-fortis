Dr. Anagha Heroor

Dr. Anagha Heroor is a Consulting Ophthalmic Surgeon at Fortis Hospital Kalyan.
Professionally, Dr. Anagha Heroor's special interests include Glaucoma, Cataract & Lasik. She has been performing Lasik surgery since the last 10 years. She has conducted many eye camps in Dombivli and adjoining rural areas of Dativili and Kamba villages. She also conducts eye camps in the schools and colleges of Dombivli, for the early detection of visual disorders in children and teenagers.
