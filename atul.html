Dr. Atul Limaye

Dr. Atul Limaye is an Interventional Cardiologist at Fortis Hospital, Mulund .He has worked at Mount Sinai Hospital in New York, USA where he was Assistant Professor at the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai and on the Cardiac catheterization lab faculty at the Mount Sinai Hospital, New York. He specializes in complex coronary and peripheral interventions including TAVR - Transcatheter Aortic Valve Replacement.
