Leading Cancer Treatment Hospital in Mumbai

Oncology is a branch of medicine dealing with the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of cancer. An oncologist specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of cancer.

At Fortis, we are committed to putting our patients first to deliver the best possible cancer care. From diagnosis to treatment to recovery, our teams of experts are here to help you and your caregivers during all stages of your cancer journey. Our department provides access to state-of-the-art therapies and comprehensive cancer care offering leading-edge medical, surgical and radiation oncology treatment options, as well as access to specialized research and diagnostic services.

The goal of any cancer treatment is to eliminate the cancer and prevent or reduce the chance of recurrence in the future. The various types of cancer therapy may be used alone or in combinations, depending on the type of cancer and other factors, such as stage of tumor, and your medical condition.


Comprehensive Cancer Support
