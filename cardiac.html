Centre of Excellence for Heart Care

The Cardiology Department at Fortis hospitals provides the best of services in terms of accurate diagnosis and professional treatment. Our experienced team of doctors specialise in cardiac bypass surgery, minimally invasive surgery, interventional cardiology, and non-invasive cardiology. We also provide ambulatory systems for emergencies along with non-invasive image processing, echocardiography, stress tests, cardiovascular CT's and thallium heart mapping among many other services. Our paediatric division also diagnoses and treats cardiac issues in newborns and foetuses alike.


The Team

The Department has a team of the top cardiologists in India supported by advanced technology and the best practices in Non-invasive cardiology, Interventional Cardiology, Electrophysiology and Paediatric Cardiology. The Department of Electrophysiology offers world-class diagnosis and treatment options for heart rhythm problems (cardiac arrhythmias). Our experienced team of doctors perform comprehensive invasive procedures for treatment of cardiac arrhythmias. In addition, we offer specialised treatments and therapies like pacemakers, defibrillators and ablations for supraventricular tachycardia (SVT), amongst others.
Fortis has a specialised multi-disciplinary team of paediatric cardiologists, paediatric surgeons, anaesthesiologists and paediatric intensivists. The team is solely dedicated to work towards child cardiac care. Several cardiac techniques for children have been pioneered by the team. The department caters to extensive echocardiographic work on children and focuses in detecting cardiac defects in unborn children using specialised echocardiography techniques
